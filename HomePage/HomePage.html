Lab Three
Description: In this lab, we had to work in partners to re-create the game, Silverjack. For this game, we had to assign each of the players a random number of random cards from 4 to 6 cards each without any of the cards repeating, we had to give the cards a number value, and then we had to add up the scores of each of the four players and determine the winner. The player with the resulting score of 42, or the score closest to 42 while still being under 42 is the winner. The program then displays the winner and gives the option of playing again with the same players, or typing in new player names.
